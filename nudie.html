INTRODUCTION

Project Context

We can all agree that this pandemic has brought devastating losses not only to ourphysical well-being but our mental health. We won't speak for everyone but, the girls at Nudie had reached a peak of anxiety, stress and isolation. We no longer could reach outand hold hands, sweat at a spin class or even kiss strangers we met on the internet.

With hours of mindlessly scrolling through social media, one thing became evidently clear- we were all struggling with our body image. This project is rooted in the notion that wewant to work towards bettering our mental health and hope to inspire the same with ourcommunity.

My expertise was eCommerce, web development, sourcing, manufacturing and eCommerce related marketing while wearing many other hats including finance and accounting, B2B development and more!
